How to sell IPv4 with Interlir

Contact our sales team and register with Interlir

  • If you want to sell an IP network, please, contact Interlir with info@interlir.com or by clicking to support button to discuss your requirements
  • Interlir provides its clients with a robust platform to help with KYC(Know your Customer) process, required to identify your company according to RIRs rules.

Negotiations and contracting

  • Interlir or Buyer, negotiates the final price for IP networks, and sign an agreement with the seller.

Escrow

  • Buyers sends funds to Escrow service and wait for a IP Network transfer

Transfer addresses and funds transfer

  • A seller transfers IP addresses with a help of Intelir. After a transfer have been confirmed with a buyer, escrow funds would been transferred to a seller.
